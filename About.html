Deanna Halls is the Communications Director of Qi Creative, an award winning pediatric healthcare agency in Alberta, Canada. She is a Professional Human Ecologist (PHEc) and Early Childhood Educator, with training in Triple P and Mental Health Intervention for youth, in addition to intensive care and recreation therapy in eldercare settings. She has penned over 100 articles around disability and human ecology under Qi Creative and the International Journal of Home Economics, attending the IFHE World Congress under a full travel scholarship.

Since learning voiceover at On The Mic in Vancouver, alongside her Master of Arts studies in Professional Communication at Royal Roads University, past projects include audio dramas (Admiral Shao Jun in The Pilgrimage Saga, Hou Lin in Tales From The Fringes of Reality), and co-hosting Qi Talks, a podcast by Qi Creative. She considers her interviewing, counseling, and intervention skills with people across all ages a great asset to voiceover.
